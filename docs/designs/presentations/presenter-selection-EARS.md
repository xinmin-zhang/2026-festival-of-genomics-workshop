# Presenter Selection - EARS

**Parent LLD**: ./LLD.md

## Selection Criteria

- **PRES-SELECT-001**: The system shall select 3 presenters who represent different domains (e.g., genomics, chemistry, adjacent field).
- **PRES-SELECT-002**: The system shall select 3 presenters who represent different approaches (e.g., slow-down-upfront, speed-then-validate, hybrid).
- **PRES-SELECT-003**: The system shall prioritize willingness to share over polish or correctness of analysis.
- **PRES-SELECT-004**: The system shall approach specific people to present rather than calling for volunteers.

## Selection Timing

- **PRES-SELECT-010**: When the hackathon reaches minute 15, the system shall begin identifying presenter candidates.
- **PRES-SELECT-011**: When the hackathon reaches minute 45, the system shall announce the 3 selected presenters.
- **PRES-SELECT-012**: After announcing presenters, the system shall give them 5 minutes to prepare slides or notes.

## Fallback

- **PRES-SELECT-020**: If fewer than 3 willing presenters are available, then the system shall invite specific people who showed promising approaches during circulation.
- **PRES-SELECT-021**: If only 2 willing presenters are available, then the system shall proceed with 2 presentations and use the remaining time for wrap-up and networking encouragement.
