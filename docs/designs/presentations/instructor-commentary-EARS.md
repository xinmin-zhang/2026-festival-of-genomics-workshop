# Instructor Commentary - EARS

**Parent LLD**: ./LLD.md

## Commentary Style

- **PRES-COMMENT-001**: The system shall frame observations as generalizable lessons: "What I noticed here is..."
- **PRES-COMMENT-002**: The system shall connect observations to the one-question-at-a-time principle: "This is exactly what we talked about — slowing down where your domain expertise says to."
- **PRES-COMMENT-003**: The system shall highlight teachable moments for the room: "For everyone else, a thing to watch for here is..."
- **PRES-COMMENT-004**: The system shall be warm and encouraging in tone.

## Commentary Boundaries

- **PRES-COMMENT-010**: The system shall not spend more than 1 minute on commentary per presenter.
- **PRES-COMMENT-011**: The system shall not give unsolicited advice that turns the presentation into a one-on-one coaching session.
- **PRES-COMMENT-012**: The system shall not focus on what went wrong without acknowledging what went right.
- **PRES-COMMENT-013**: The system shall not be critical or embarrassing (not a "Simon Cowell" approach).

## Handling Missed Steps

- **PRES-COMMENT-020**: If a presenter missed an important check, then the system shall frame it as a teachable moment: "A teachable moment for the room — when working backwards, it's easy to skip X. Here's why checking it matters..."
- **PRES-COMMENT-021**: If an audience member spots a missed step, then the system shall engage the audience: "Good catch — what would you have done differently?"
- **PRES-COMMENT-022**: If the presenter is aware of and addressed a potential miss, then the system shall acknowledge it positively: "Nice — you caught that yourself, which is exactly the skill we're building."
- **PRES-COMMENT-023**: If a miss is minor and does not affect conclusions, then the system shall acknowledge it briefly and move on: "One thing to keep in mind for next time..."

## Wrap-up

- **PRES-COMMENT-030**: After the final presentation, the system shall thank everyone for coming.
- **PRES-COMMENT-031**: After the final presentation, the system shall encourage networking and organic follow-up conversations.
- **PRES-COMMENT-032**: The system shall remind presenters that the instructor offers career development chats to them.
