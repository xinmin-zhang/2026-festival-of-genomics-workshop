# Hackathon Instructor Role - EARS

**Parent LLD**: ./LLD.md

## Setup Support

- **HACK-INSTR-001**: When the hackathon begins, the system shall provide setup support for uv and marimo installation.
- **HACK-INSTR-002**: When a participant has a setup issue, the system shall help them resolve it.
- **HACK-INSTR-003**: If a participant has a setup issue that cannot be resolved quickly, then the system shall provide a fallback dataset option.

## During Working Time

- **HACK-INSTR-010**: During the 40-minute working period, the system shall not circulate to debug analysis code or help with Marimo usage.
- **HACK-INSTR-011**: During the 40-minute working period, the system shall circulate to observe participant approaches.
- **HACK-INSTR-012**: During the 40-minute working period, the system shall identify participants with diverse domains and approaches for presentation selection.

## Presenter Selection

- **HACK-INSTR-020**: When selecting presenters (by minute 45), the system shall choose 3 participants based on diversity of domain and approach, not volunteer status.
- **HACK-INSTR-021**: The system shall start identifying presenter candidates by minute 15 of the hackathon.
- **HACK-INSTR-022**: The system shall approach specific people to present rather than calling for volunteers.
- **HACK-INSTR-023**: The system shall give selected presenters at least 5 minutes to prepare before their presentation.

## Success Indicators

- **HACK-INSTR-030**: The system shall look for participants who slow down intentionally and ask questions before writing code.
- **HACK-INSTR-031**: The system shall look for participants who validate results before proceeding to the next step.
- **HACK-INSTR-032**: The system shall look for participants who engage critically with agent output rather than accepting it blindly.
- **HACK-INSTR-033**: The system shall look for participants who can articulate their domain reasoning: "In my field, X matters, so I need to check Y."
- **HACK-INSTR-034**: The system shall look for participants who practice reproducibility: agent pulls data from source URLs, no hardcoded paths, uv-compatible.
